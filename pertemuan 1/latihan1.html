Films with Sam Siegel Productions

The Bridge on the River Kwai (1957)

Produces by Sam Siegel, this film was the first of the Lean blockbuster movies, and featured a young Alec Guinnes, William Holden, and a brilliant performance from Sessue Hayakawa.

Lawrencr of Arabia (1962)

One of my all-time favorite movies, this epic adventure starring Peter O'Toole established Lean as a director who could truly envision film on a grand scale.
